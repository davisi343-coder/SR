Rifle

Starting at A or C engage all 8 rifle targets in a continuous 2,1,1,1,1,1,1,2 sweep. (R1,R1,R2,R3,R4, move to other position, R5,R6,R7,R8,R8)

Pistol

Sweep from either end with a double tap on the first target then single tap the next 3, then in the same direction, single tap the first three targets then double tap the last. tap.(P1,P1,P2,P3,P4,P1,P2,P3,P4,P4)
